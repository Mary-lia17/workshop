At Brainstorm, we envision a future where cultural heritage and climate resilience grow together
— where the knowledge of the past becomes a compass for a sustainable tomorrow. Our goal is to
create a living bridge between tradition and innovation, ensuring that the cultural identity of
the Gulf remains vibrant, adaptive, and accessible for generations to come. We believe that heritage
preservation is not only about safeguarding what once was, but about nurturing what still lives —
in local crafts, oral traditions, architecture, and the landscapes that shape Gulf life. In an era of
environmental change, protecting this legacy means reimagining how we teach, create, and collaborate.

OUR MAIN GOALS

- Empowering Local Communities:

Every project begins and grows with the people who live closest to heritage sites.
Their knowledge, experience, and creativity are the foundation of our preservation efforts.

- Education as the Heart of Preservation:

We see education as the most powerful tool for lasting change. By introducing cultural and
environmental awareness from childhood, and embedding it within university programs,
Brainstorm cultivates future generations of artists, scholars, and citizens who understand
the deep interconnection between culture, nature, and climate.

- Connecting Art and Research:

Artists across the Gulf act as ambassadors of awareness — traveling, exhibiting, and engaging
communities on how climate change affects their heritage. Through partnerships with universities
and research institutes, these creative expressions are paired with academic inquiry and scientific
innovation, turning knowledge into tangible action.

- Fostering Global Dialogue:

Through exchange programs, collaborative research, and international partnerships, Brainstorm
builds bridges between Gulf universities and institutions around the world. These connections
enrich local expertise and position the Gulf as a hub of sustainable cultural research and innovation.

- Innovation and Accessibility:

We embrace technology to make heritage accessible to all. From 3D digital preservation and virtual
exhibitions to creative merchandising that supports local artists, Brainstorm combines
tradition and technology to keep cultural memory alive and relevant.
