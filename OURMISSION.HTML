Our mission

Brainstorm is a Gulf-based initiative dedicated to safeguarding the cultural heritage
of the Arab Gulf countries in the face of climate change. We work to protect traditions,
crafts, and local knowledge as vital foundations for a sustainable and resilient future.
Through a bottom-up approach, we empower local communities, artists, and researchers
to lead preservation efforts and reimagine how heritage can thrive in a changing environment.
Education lies at the heart of our mission — from creative labs in schools that nurture cultural
awareness among children, to academic partnerships that connect Gulf universities with
institutions worldwide. By uniting art, education, and research, Brainstorm transforms heritage
preservation into a living, collaborative practice — one that strengthens identity, inspires
innovation, and builds resilience across generations.
